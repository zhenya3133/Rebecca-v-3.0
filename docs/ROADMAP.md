## Progress Control & Quality Assurance

### Daily Control Points
- **Code Quality**: Automated linting, testing, and security scans
- **Build Status**: CI/CD pipeline monitoring and alerts
- **Performance**: Response time and resource usage metrics
- **Error Tracking**: Exception monitoring and resolution time

### Weekly Reviews
- **Sprint Progress**: Task completion rates and blockers
- **Quality Metrics**: Test coverage, bug count, technical debt
- **Performance Benchmarks**: Load testing and optimization
- **Security Audits**: Vulnerability scans and compliance checks

### Monthly Assessments
- **Feature Delivery**: Roadmap milestone completion
- **Technical Debt**: Code refactoring and architecture improvements
- **Market Feedback**: User surveys and feature requests
- **Competitive Analysis**: Market positioning and feature gaps

### Quarterly Business Reviews
- **Revenue Metrics**: Customer acquisition and retention
- **Product-Market Fit**: Usage analytics and customer satisfaction
- **Technical Strategy**: Architecture decisions and technology stack
- **Team Performance**: Resource allocation and skill development

## Risk Management & Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Scalability bottlenecks**: Proactive performance testing and optimization

- **Technology obsolescence**: Continuous technology evaluation and updates

### Business Risks
- **Market competition**: Unique value proposition and rapid innovation
- **Customer acquisition**: Strong product-market fit and referral programs
- **Regulatory changes**: Compliance monitoring and adaptation strategies

- **Phase 1**: 70% development, 20% infrastructure, 10% operations
- **Phase 2**: 60% development, 25% infrastructure, 15% operations
- **Phase 3**: 40% development, 30% infrastructure, 30% business
- **Phase 4**: 30% development, 35% infrastructure, 35% business

This roadmap provides a structured approach to developing Rebecca Platform from a technical prototype to a commercially viable product. Regular reviews and adjustments will ensure alignment with market demands and technological evolution.
